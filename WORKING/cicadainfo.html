As Spring heads towards Summer, and the sounds of cicadas fill the morning and afternoon air, Bream and Bass in tree lined streams and rivers turn their attention to the surface in search of live food. There is no better time to fish with a cicada fizzer lure.
 

The Koolabung 35mm and 45mm Cicada Fizzer lures are formed in a mould cast from a cicada body, and painted in a fish attracting range of colours created to suit various water colour and ambient light conditions.

Extensively tested by recreational and competition anglers, these lures consistently catch good bags of quality Bream and Bass.
